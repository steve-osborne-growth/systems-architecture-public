# Component: Seller Enablement

## Purpose

The seller enablement component ensures that the sales team understands the EMS transition, trusts the data, and has the tools to act on it. Without seller buy-in, the migration does not generate revenue.

---

## What It Includes

- **Office hours and training sessions:** Regular sessions framed around revenue opportunity rather than compliance burden. Sellers are more likely to engage when they understand the upsell potential.
- **Data tools in Google Sheets:** Familiar interface where sellers can review their accounts, see overage details, and identify outreach priorities without navigating Domo directly.
- **Communication playbooks:** Guidance on how to approach customers about overages in a way that leads to upsells rather than complaints.
- **Manager briefings:** Separate training for sales managers so they can reinforce the initiative with their teams.

---

## Design Notes

Enablement is treated as infrastructure in this system, not as a one-time training event. The migration spans months and affects every seller differently depending on their accounts. Ongoing support, repeated communication, and accessible tools are more effective than a single training deck.

The decision to use Google Sheets as the seller-facing data tool was deliberate. Sellers are already comfortable with Sheets. Introducing a new platform would have added adoption friction at the worst possible time.
