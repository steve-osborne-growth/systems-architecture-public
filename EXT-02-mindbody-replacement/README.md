# MindBody Replacement: Custom Booking and Licensing System

A fully custom booking, membership, and session licensing platform built to replace a $900/month SaaS product for a personal training business.

---

## The Problem

A personal training business was paying approximately $900 per month for MindBody, a popular booking platform for fitness businesses. The software never fully fit how the business actually operated. Group classes, one-on-one sessions, virtual training, packages, punch cards, and memberships all had to coexist in one system. MindBody handled some of it but not all of it, and the cost was high for a small business with three employees.

The goal was to replace it entirely with a custom-built system that cost only the price of hosting and gave the owner complete control.

---

## Outcomes

- Fully replaced MindBody at a fraction of the cost
- All booking types supported: group classes, one-on-one sessions, virtual training, packages, punch cards, and monthly memberships
- Automatic session cap enforcement prevents overbooking against purchased packages
- Customers manage their own accounts, bookings, and package history
- Runs with minimal maintenance
- Business has continued to grow smoothly as new employees and customers were added
