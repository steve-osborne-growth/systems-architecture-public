# Automated Google Business Profile Posting System

A pre-AI content automation system that reduced manual posting work from 16 hours per employee per month to 30 minutes, generating $24,000 in monthly recurring revenue within six months.

*Built before large language models existed. The approach predates what AI does today by design.*

---

## The Problem

An SEO agency was manually creating Google Business Profile posts for apartment complex clients. The work involved researching each property, writing unique posts about amenities and features, sourcing matching photos, and scheduling posts on a regular cadence. Some clients wanted daily posts.

This was consuming 16 hours per employee per month and was not scalable. The service was in demand and clients were willing to pay for it, but the manual labor required made it impossible to grow profitably.

---

## Outcomes

- Manual posting time reduced from 16 hours per employee per month to approximately 30 minutes
- $24,000 in monthly recurring revenue generated within six months of launch
- System ran and powered the entire revenue stream until large language models made the approach obsolete
- Posts sounded like natural language and matched photos correctly, which was the core quality challenge
