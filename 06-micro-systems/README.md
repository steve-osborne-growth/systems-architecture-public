# Micro Systems

Small, targeted systems built to solve specific operational problems quickly. Each one was designed, built, and shipped by one person. Most took hours, not weeks.

These are not large architectural platforms. They are pragmatic tools that removed friction, saved time, and in many cases directly influenced revenue. The pattern across all of them is the same: see a problem, build the fix, ship it.

---

## Systems

| System | Problem Solved | Tech | Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| [License Assignment Validation Tool](./license-assignment-validation/) | No way to audit which users counted toward billing without manual PDF review | Google Apps Script, JavaScript, Trimble Assistant | Eliminated daily manual review requests for 18 months until a permanent solution was built |
| [Customer Communication Library](./customer-communication-library/) | Seller communication history scattered across Gmail and Gong with no way to consolidate | Gmail API, Gong API, Google Apps Script | Full customer knowledge base built in minutes instead of impossible to compile manually |
| [Operational Contact Gathering System](./operational-contact-gathering/) | Gathering customer contacts required six hours of nonstop manual report editing | Salesforce CLI, SOQL, Google Apps Script, PowerShell | Six hours of manual work reduced to five minutes. 20,000+ contacts from 5,500 accounts. |
| [Marketo/Mailchimp Stopgap](./marketo-mailchimp-stopgap/) | Zero ability to contact customers at scale for six months after Marketo was decommissioned | Google Apps Script, JavaScript, Gmail API | Restored bulk customer communication during a critical gap. Reduced billing disputes. |
| [Seller Email Generator](./seller-email-generator/) | Sellers manually building customer outreach one by one from raw dashboard data | Google Sheets, Google Apps Script, Gmail API, Domo | Bulk personalized email drafts generated in seconds instead of hours |
