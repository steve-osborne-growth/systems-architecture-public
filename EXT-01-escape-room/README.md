# King's Escape Room: Business Operating System

Designing and building every system required to take an escape room business from owner-operator to autonomous operation, then selling it.

---

## The Business

King's Escape Room launched in South Denver, Colorado as one of the first escape rooms in the market. It grew to six rooms, seven employees, and over $300,000 in annual revenue with high profit margins. The business dominated the local Denver market in organic search, which was its primary customer acquisition channel.

The business was eventually sold in full, including all digital assets and the SEO infrastructure that drove traffic.

---

## The Real Project

The escape room was not the product. The operating system was the product.

The rooms, the puzzles, the technology, the staff training, the marketing: all of it had to be systematized well enough that the business could run without the owner in the building. That transition from owner-operator to absent owner is where most small businesses fail. Getting it right required designing and documenting every system from the ground up.

This project documents those systems.

---

## Outcome

- Seven employees at peak
- $300,000+ annual revenue with high profit margins
- Six rooms designed and built end to end
- Business ran without owner involvement for over a year before sale
- Sold for full asset value including digital and marketing infrastructure
