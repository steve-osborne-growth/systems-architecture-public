# Component: Puzzle Design and Game Flow

## Purpose

Design the end-to-end customer experience inside each room, including puzzle logic, branching game flow, pacing, and difficulty calibration.

---

## What It Did

Six rooms were designed and built from scratch over the life of the business. Each room had a complete puzzle flow designed before any physical construction began. The flow mapped which puzzles unlocked which other puzzles, how the difficulty ramped across the session, and where natural pacing moments existed for the customer.

Early escape rooms used linear puzzle flows: solve puzzle A to get to puzzle B, then puzzle C. This became predictable and less engaging as the industry matured. King's Escape Room moved to branching flows where multiple puzzle paths ran simultaneously. Customers could work in parallel, which accommodated different group sizes and skill sets and made the experience feel more like a real environment than a test.

The 60-minute target was designed to feel like a near-miss rather than a completion. The goal was for every customer to finish the room, even if they went over time, rather than being pulled out mid-puzzle. Feedback consistently showed that customers who finished, even past the time limit, rated the experience higher than those who were stopped mid-progress.

---

## Built With

- Laser cutting for precision puzzle components
- Custom construction: cutting walls, installing hidden compartments and doors
- Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop for custom graphics, symbols, and visual puzzle elements
- Prop sourcing and retrofitting from thrift stores and retail across the Denver metro area
- Physical prototyping and playtesting with groups before each room opened

---

## Impact

The puzzle design was the product. Every other system in the business existed to support and protect the customer experience the puzzles created. Investing in branching, non-linear game flow differentiated King's Escape Room from simpler competitors and drove repeat visits and positive reviews that fed the SEO system.
