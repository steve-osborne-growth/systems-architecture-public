# Component: Knowledge Base

## Purpose

The knowledge base component ensures that every person who interacts with the on-demand program, whether a seller, customer success manager, or support agent, has accurate, accessible information and does not need to rely on a central person to answer routine questions.

---

## What It Includes

**Internal Google Site**
A structured internal website built on Google Sites that serves as the single source of truth for the program. It includes program overview, how billing works, seller playbooks, process documentation, and answers to common questions. All training materials link back to the site.

**AI Chatbot**
Built using Trimble Assistant and validated over hundreds of iterations. The chatbot answers questions about the on-demand program in natural language. It was tested extensively to ensure it never produced incorrect answers, since misinformation spread to sellers or customers would directly impact revenue and trust.

The validation process involved running the chatbot through common question patterns, edge cases, and known areas of confusion, then correcting responses iteratively until output was reliable across all scenarios.

**Training Materials**
Structured training content for sellers, their managers, customer success teams, and customer support. Each audience has a tailored version that focuses on what they need to know to do their job in relation to the program.

---

## Why This Is a First-Class Component

A program that sellers do not understand does not generate revenue. In the early stages of rebuilding this program, technical infrastructure improved faster than seller awareness and confidence. Revenue growth was limited by adoption, not by system capability.

Treating enablement as a core system component rather than an afterthought changed that. The knowledge base and chatbot reduced inbound support questions significantly and gave sellers the confidence to act on program data without waiting for clarification.
